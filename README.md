## What is GlyphOS?

GlyphOS is a **web-based operating system** that gives you a full desktop experience right in your browser. Think of it as having your own personal computer that:

- ✨ **Lives in the cloud** - Access from any device, anywhere
- 🤝 **Syncs in real-time** - Collaborate with others instantly
- 🎨 **Works offline** - Everything saves locally, no internet required
- 🔒 **Keeps your data private** - Your files never leave your device (unless you share them)
- 🚀 **Runs everywhere** - Windows, Mac, Linux, tablets, phones

## 🚀 Getting Started (3 Easy Steps)

### 1. Open GlyphOS
Visit **[glyphd.com](https://glyphd.com)** in any modern browser

### 2. Explore Your Desktop
- Click the **⊞ Apps** button (bottom left) to see all available apps
- Click the **📁 Files** button to browse your files
- Click the **🏪 Market** button to discover new apps

### 3. Start Creating
- Launch **Notes** to start writing
- Open **Canvas** to start drawing
- Try **Live Collab** to work with friends

**That's it!** Everything auto-saves. Your work is always there when you return.
